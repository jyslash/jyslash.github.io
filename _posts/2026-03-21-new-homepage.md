Hello internet! Finally I pulled the trigger and launched personal homepage, thanks to the FOMO of vibe coding. There were a few things that I wanted to do things differently this time: 1) It's not a portfolio site, hence my audience is my friends. 2) posting should be the main surface, and I should be able to easily post it. 3) Jekyll static website should be the way since this felt the most straight and direct way of managing my own thoughts. 

To be honest, moving away from Portfolio showcase format gives me quite a freedom that I don't need to try too much to upsell my work. I'll just keep logging my life here and there, and I hope you like it. One bonus is that I can also practice casual English writing as a regular basis! 95% of my writing is tied to work and I really need to expand the topics and style. Meaning you'll see a lot of errors and unnatural expression - please bear with me!
